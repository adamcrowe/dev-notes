Bootstrap 3 Collapse

Collapsibles are useful when you want to hide and show large amount of content

Tip: Plugins can be included individually (using Bootstrap's individual "collapse.js" file), or all at once (using "bootstrap.js" or "bootstrap.min.js"). Plugin dependency: Collapse requires the transitions plugin to be included in your version of Bootstrap.
